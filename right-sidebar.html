Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do the Topmate checkout milestones work?

A: When you click to purchase any of our comprehensive study packages, you will pay exactly 50% of the package cost as an upfront project deposit via Topmate. The remaining half is requested manually via a custom checkout link only after your official acceptance letter arrives.

Q: What if I want to apply to multiple distinct regions?

A: Our primary packages scale seamlessly. You can expand a single-country framework or insert an additional program focus directly on your consulting line for an incremental add-on rate of €250 per country or €100 per program.

Q: How do we coordinate document reviews?

A: Once your package check-in is complete, you will gain private text access via Topmate Priority DMs. You can submit your drafts asynchronously at any time, and you will receive line-by-line structural edits or detailed video critiques within a 3-day turnaround window.
