Our Service Commitment & Financial Framework

Clear, transparent operation guidelines governing your milestone consulting packages

We believe elite consulting requires deep structural accountability. To ensure a completely fair partnership, our regional comprehensive study packages operate under a transparent milestone framework built around your admissions success. Below are the definitive structural rules that apply when you lock in a package or add-on on our platform.

1. The 50/50 Milestone Payment Mechanism

All premium Regional Study Packages (covering USA, Canada, UK, Singapore, Australia, New Zealand, or European options) split the financial commitment into two equal phases:

  • Phase 1 (Upfront Deposit): A 50% upfront payment is processed via Topmate to initiate your profile evaluation, document restructuring, and university shortlisting stages.
  • Phase 2 (Success Milestone): The remaining 50% remainder is due only after you secure an official admission offer from at least one of your targeted programs.

2. Our Success Guarantee & Refund Policy

We strictly limit our active client rosters to dedicate maximum strategic energy to each application profile. If your application cycle across your managed university options is entirely unsuccessful (meaning zero admission offers are obtained), 50% of your Phase 1 upfront deposit will be refunded to you directly.

Please note: Standalone Masterclasses, Priority DM document reviews, and direct add-on program fees require full payment upfront and are non-refundable.

3. Program Scalability & Add-On Fees

Our baseline regional application management structures map out a strategic target list capped at 10 universities. For individual scholarship packages or corporate internship support programs, you can expand a single-country framework or insert an additional program focus directly on your consulting line for an incremental add-on rate of €250 per country or €100 per program.

4. External Costs Exclusion

Our consulting fees cover exclusively our strategic time, editing pipelines, live review call blocks, and advisory services. Third-party transaction expenses—including institutional university application fees, standardized testing costs, translation fees, and visa processing payments—must be met directly by the client to the relevant body.
